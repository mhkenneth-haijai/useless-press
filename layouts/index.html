From the desk of the editor

This is a hobbyist's notebook. We follow what catches our eye — cameras, cars, motorcycles, films, the occasional piece of gadgetry that has no business being as beautiful as it is. Research is honest about what we don't know; commentary is honest about what we do. Twice a week, sometimes less, never more.

— Haijai, the editor.
